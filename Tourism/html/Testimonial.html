With Us

we are together

We, Dream Lanka Tours founded in 2007, are located in Colombo in the capital of Sri Lanka. We work as an Inbound Tour Operator and organize very interesting cultural tours and beach holidays only in Sri Lanka. During the past few years we have organized many holiday packages and satisfied hundreds of guests from different parts of the world. Please do have a look at Our Gallery page to view some testimonials. Our prime objective is to satisfy our clients to the best of our ability through dedication and hard work. We have a modern fleet of vehicles and employ English speaking and Italian speaking tour guides . Please contact us and just tell your requirements. We may build up your tour plan together. You may request pricing information for any tour package without any obligation of purchase.
